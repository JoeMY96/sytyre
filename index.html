ABOUT US

Your Leading Tyre Supplier Since 2010

Dedicated to Quality and Comprehensive Tyre Solutions

Established in 2010, our company has emerged as a leading tire supplier. We offer a comprehensive range of tires to meet diverse needs. Our product lineup includes HP tires for high-performance driving, off-road tires designed for rough terrains, and light truck tires suitable for light commercial vehicles.

For cold-weather conditions, we have winter tires, while drifting tires are available for sport enthusiasts. Additionally, we provide electric vehicle tires tailored to the unique requirements of EVs. Our portfolio also extends to motorcycle tires, agricultural tires, and inner tubes. With a commitment to quality and a wide product selection, we strive to be the one-stop solution for all tire needs in the market.
